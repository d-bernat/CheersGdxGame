The drinking game ,,Bus Driving,, will be played in 3 phases.
1. Phase
Every player will become 4 cards but need to answer some questions on the screen bevor. Then you can turn your card face up. Is your answer is wrong than you need to drink. Is your answer right than is the next persons turn.
2. Phase
In the 2. phase you'll see an pyramid. In the first row you can despite one sip, in the second row two sips and so on. You don't need to lay down a card, you can wait and lay it down later, maybe you can despites more sips than. The player which has the most cards at the end of the game have to drive the bus. If more than one player have the same number of cards there will be a overtime and one after another will choose if the card is higher or lower than the card on the screen.
3. Phase
Now you can drive the bus and you need to guess if the next card will be higher or lower than the card on the screen. Are you right you will move on and are you wrong you will drink and return to start.
