Agulone

Type
Longdrink, Middle

Glass
Longdrinkglass

Ingredients
1 piece of cucumber
5cl Vodka
2cl Melon juice
2cl lemon juice
6cl Apple juice

Preparation
1. Cut the cucumber in pieces and mix it.
2. Fill the other ingredients into 
   a mixer and mix it too.
3. Fill the content with a sieve into the glass.
4. Cheers!
