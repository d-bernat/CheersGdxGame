Raspberry

Type
Longdrink, Light

Glass
Longdrinkglass

Ingredients
7 pieces of raspberry
1cl Elderflower Syrup
1cl Lime Juice
4cl Champagne Rose
4cl Vodka

Preparation
1. Fill Ice and raspberry into your glass.
2. Mix all ingredients except of champagne.
3. Fill the content into your glass.
4. Add champagne.
5. Cheers!
