Exotic Rain

Typ
Longdrink, Light

Glass
Longdrinkglass

Zutaten
4cl Vodka
2cl Mango syrup
100ml Mango juice
8 raspberries

Zubereitung
1. Fill ice cubes into your glass.
2. Mix vodka, mango juice and syrup 
   and fill it inot your glass too.
3. Mix the raspberries into a puree.
4. Fill the content softly at the 
   top of your drink.
5. Cheers!
