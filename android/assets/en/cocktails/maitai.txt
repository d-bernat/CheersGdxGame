Mai Tai

Type
Longdrink, Strong

Glass
Tumbler

Ingredients
3cl Jamaica Rum
3cl Martinique Rum
8ml Candy syrup
8ml Almond syrup
2cl Dry Orange Curacao
2cl Lime Juice

Preparation
1. Mix all ingredients together.
2. Fill the whole glass with crushed ice.
3. Fill now the content in your glass.
4. Cheers!
