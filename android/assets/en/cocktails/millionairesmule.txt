Millionaires Mule

Type
Classic, Strong

Glass
Longdrinkglass

Ingredients
2cl Gin
2 slides of lime
2 slides of ginger
2cl sugar syrup
10cl Champagne

Preparation
1. Fill Ginger and sugar syrup
   into your glass.
2. Give the limes into it.
3. Fill 1/2 of the glass with ice cubes.
4. Add gin.
5. Add champagne.
6. Mix it once.
7. Cheers!
