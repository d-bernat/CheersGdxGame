Mojito

Type
Classic, Middle

Glass
Longdrinkglass

Ingredients
4cl Rum white
2cl Lime Juice
2tL Cane sugar
2 Mint leaves
330ml Soda Water

Zubereitung
1. Fill mint, lime juice
   and sugar into your glass.
2. Fill the whole glass with
   crushed iced.
3. Add rum.
4. Mix it all with soda water.
5. Cheers!
