Lila Caipirinha

Type
Classic, Middle

Glass
Tumbler

Ingredients
1 piece of lime
2TSP cane sugar white 
1 Splash Lime Juice
6cl Livo-Liva

Preparation
1. Fill your glass with crushed ice.
2. Cut the lime in pieces and put 
   it into your glass.
3. Give cane sugar, Lime Juice
   and Livo-Liva into it.
4. Mix it.
5. Cheers!
