Sex on the beach

Type
Classic, Middle

Glass
Longdrinkglass

Ingredients
4cl Vodka
2cl Peach Liqueur
4cl Orange Juice
4cl Cranberry Juice

Preparation
1. Mix all ingredients with Ice.
2. Fill the content into your glass.
3. If you want you can decode it 
   with cherries and pieces of 
   pine apple.
4. Cheers!
