Long Island Ice Tea

Type
Longdrink, Strong

Glass
Longdrinkglass

Ingredients
2cl Vodka
2cl Rum White
2cl Gin
2cl Triple Sec
2cl Sugar Syrup
2cl Lemon Juice
330ml Coca Cola

Preparation
1. Fill all ingredients except 
   Coca Cola into your glass.
2. Mix it.
3. Fill the coke now into it.
3. Give some ice cubes there.
4. Cheers!
