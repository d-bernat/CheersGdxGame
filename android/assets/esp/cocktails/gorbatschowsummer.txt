Gorbatschow Summer

Type
Longdrink, Light

Glass
Collinsglass

Ingredients
1cl Strawberry juice
2cl Cranberry juice
4cl Orange nectar
6cl Soda Water
1cl Strawberry syrup
3cl Vodka Gorbatschow

Preparation
1. Fill 1/2 of your glass with ice.
2. Give strawberry syrup and juice to it.
3. Fill cranberry juice into it.
4. Fill Orange nectar and Soda Water into it.
5. Add Vodka.
6. Shake it.
7. Cheers!
