Lady Rose

Type
Classic, Middle

Glass
Longdrinkglass

Ingredients
2cl Monin Rosensirup
4cl Gin
200ml Russian Wildberry
4 ice cubes
2 slices of lemon

Preparation
1. Fill your glass with ice cubes.
2. Give all ingredients into it and shake it.
5. Decode your drink with 2 slices of lemon.
6. Cheers!
