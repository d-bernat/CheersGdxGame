Fresh-Up

Type
Sparkling Wine, Light

Glass
Wineglass

Ingredients
2cl Sanddorn liqueur
5ml Lime juice
5ml Apricot syrup
8cl Sparkling Wine Rose

Preparation
1. Mix all ingredients together exept 
   the sparkling wine.
2. Put 3 ice cubes into the glass.
3. Fill the content into your glass. 
4. Now you can fill the sparkling 
   wine into it.
5. Cheers!
